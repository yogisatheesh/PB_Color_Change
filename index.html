About me
"Your motivation lies between what you already have and what you want to achieve."

— Pricilla Bilavendran

I've been testing software for over a decade — not just finding bugs, but championing the culture of quality. I believe great QA is invisible: when done right, users never know we existed.

Today I speak at global conferences, build communities, and mentor the next generation of testers — especially women breaking into tech.
